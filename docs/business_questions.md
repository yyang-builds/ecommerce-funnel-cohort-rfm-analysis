# Business Questions

## Objective
Use `bigquery-public-data.thelook_ecommerce` to understand how users move from browsing to purchase, how often they come back, which customer segments matter most, and which channels, browsers, and geographies drive the strongest business outcomes.

## Funnel Questions
- How many sessions progress from product view to cart to purchase?
- Where is the largest drop-off in the commerce funnel?
- Do conversion rates differ by `traffic_source` or `browser`?
- Which traffic sources generate the highest session-to-purchase rates?

## Retention Questions
- What percentage of first-time buyers return in month 1, month 2, and later months?
- How quickly does retention decay after the first purchase?
- Which cohorts are strongest or weakest over time?
- How concentrated is revenue among repeat buyers versus one-time buyers?

## Customer Value Questions
- Which RFM segments contribute the most revenue?
- How large is the `Champions` segment versus the `At Risk` segment?
- What share of customers are one-time buyers?
- Which segments should the business prioritize for retention campaigns?

## Channel, Device, and Geo Questions
- Which traffic sources bring the most purchasing users and revenue?
- Which browsers show stronger session purchase rates?
- Which countries contribute the most orders and revenue?
- Are there large differences in average order value by geography?

## Decision-Oriented Questions
- Should the business prioritize cart recovery or top-of-funnel acquisition?
- Which customer segments deserve loyalty, win-back, or onboarding campaigns?
- Which acquisition sources are strongest enough to scale?
- Which geographies should receive more localized merchandising or lifecycle messaging?
